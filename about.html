Biography

Dr. Elena Vasquez is an associate professor of neurobiology whose laboratory develops cutting-edge optical imaging technologies to uncover the dynamic interplay between neural circuits and cerebral vasculature. Her interdisciplinary research program bridges physics, engineering, and neuroscience to produce quantitative insights into how metabolic demand shapes neural computation in health and disease. Dr. Vasquez has authored more than 120 peer-reviewed publications and has been recognized with the NIH Pioneer Award, the Sloan Research Fellowship, and the McKnight Technological Innovations in Neuroscience Award.
